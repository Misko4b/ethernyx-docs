# 📈 Analytics

<div align="left"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

Analytics has four tabs — **Profile**, **Collections**, **Wallet Flips**, and **Users Flips** — plus a **Flex** card for showing off your best trades.

## Profile

Your own trading stats, pulled from every wallet on your account. Six cards up top:

* **Total Flips** — completed buy→sell pairs.
* **Success Rate** — share of flips that came out green.
* **Total Profit** — summed P&L across every flip, in ETH.
* **Avg Profit %** — average return per flip.
* **Total Volume** — everything you've bought and sold, added up.
* **Avg Hold Time** — how long you sit on an NFT before selling.

Below that: **Trading Activity** (total buys vs. sells), **Performance** (your best and worst flip), and time filters — **1D / 7D / 30D / 90D / All**, or a custom range.

The **Sales & Flips** table lists every trade — each flip shows buy and sell price, profit in ETH and %, hold time, and marketplace icons (with an arrow when you bought on one marketplace and sold on the other). **Flips by Collection** groups it all so you can see which collections actually pay.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Cross-wallet flips count.** Buy an NFT on wallet A, move it to wallet B, sell it from B — Ethernyx still stitches it into one flip and counts the profit correctly.
{% endhint %}

## Collections

Live stats on any collection you care about. Search and add collections, then see:

* **Floor price** and **top bid** — split by Blur (orange) and OpenSea (blue).
* **Floor profit (after fees)** — what you'd clear buying at the top bid and dumping at floor.
* **Supply**, contract address, and marketplace links.

Sort by name, profit, floor, or supply, and flip between **grid** and **list** view. Click any collection for the full breakdown — chain, NFT type, whether collection/trait offers are enabled, creator fees, and a sales chart.

## Wallet Flips

The same flip breakdown as Profile, but for **any wallets you want** — up to **10 at once**, yours or someone else's. Paste addresses, hit **Analyze**, and scout how a trader performs: profit, success rate, hold times, best flips, by-collection breakdown.

Cross-wallet flips and **Blur Blend** are supported here too. (No Flex share button on this tab — it's for scouting, not flexing someone else's numbers.)

## Users Flips

A leaderboard of flips across **every Ethernyx user** for the period you pick. See how the platform as a whole is trading — top flips, volumes, and by-collection performance. Public, aggregated numbers, refreshed regularly.

## Flex

Landed a fat flip? Hit **Share** on any profitable flip to generate a clean image — profit in ETH and %, hold time, the collection, buy/sell prices, your nickname and avatar. Copy it to your clipboard or download the PNG and post it wherever you flex.
