# 📈 Analytics

<div align="left"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

On the analytics page you can see 3 blocks: Profile, Collections, Wallet Flips<br>

### Profile:

Detailed statistics on all your added wallets.

### Collections:

Detailed statistics on all collections

### Wallet Flips:

Track detailed statistics for any wallets (up to 10 at once). If an NFT was purchased with one wallet, then transferred to another and sold from there, it will still be accurately counted as a flip in your analytics. Blur Blend supported as well.
