# Managing Tasks

The **Tasks** tab is where your listing tasks live. It works just like the [Collection Bidder's task list](../collection-bidder/managing-tasks.md) — minus import/export, which listings don't use.

## The toolbar

* **Search** — filter by task name, slug, contract, or token ID.
* **Status filter** — `All` · `Active` · `Inactive`.
* **Sort** — by created/updated time, slug, or marketplace.
* **Group by** — `None` · `Status` · `Marketplace` · `Group` · `Collection` · `Wallet`.
* **Select All** and **Add Task**.

## Groups

Organize listings into groups — **create**, **rename**, and **delete** them from the groups row. Deleting a group also deletes the tasks inside it (you'll get a confirmation first). Click a group to filter to it; **All Tasks** shows everything.

## Selecting & bulk actions

Click cards to select them (or **Select All**), and a bulk bar appears:

* **Start** / **Stop** the selected tasks
* **Move** them to a group
* **Delete** them
* **Clear** the selection

### Bulk edit

Change fields across many listings at once. Each field has its own checkbox — only what you tick gets applied. For listings you can bulk-edit **wallet**, **marketplace**, and **minimum price**. (Token ID and trait filter are per-NFT, so they're not bulk-editable.) Active tasks restart automatically with the new settings.

## Task cards

Each card shows the collection, your **minimum price**, the live **floor** and **top bid** (Blur / OpenSea), **floor profit**, marketplace, and token — plus start/stop, edit, live logs, delete, and details. The card tints **red** or **amber** when a limit (like your minimum price sitting above the floor) is holding the listing back, so you can spot stuck tasks at a glance.
